Rules

The Rules of War

The Goal

Win the entire deck of cards.

Setup

A full 52-card deck is dealt evenly to two players.

Play & Winning

Each player draws their top card and places it face up. Whoever has the highest-ranked card wins both cards back to their deck. Repeat until a player has won all 52 cards (meaning their opponent has 0 cards). The player with all the cards has won!

Card Ranks

Cards are ranked in descending order as such: Ace, King, Queen, Jack, 10, 9, 8, 7, 6, 5, 4, 3, 2.

War

In the event of a tie (i.e. two cards of the same rank), a War ensues. In the War, each player plays their next card facedown. They each then play a second card faceup. The higher-ranked card wins all the cards in the war. If the next cards played are of equal rank, the War repeats. The player with the higher-ranked card wins every card played in the War. For example, if there is only one tie, the winner of the War takes in six cards; if there are two ties, they take in ten; three ties, fourteen, and so on.
